# Assaf Kipnis - AUDHD Operating Profile

> Built from 6 feedback memories, CLAUDE.md rules, commands.md system design, and direct behavioral observation across multiple sessions.

## What "Actionable" Means (Specifically)

1. A piece of text he can select, copy, and paste into a text field without editing
2. A link he can click to open the right page
3. A checklist where each item is a single physical action
4. NOT: a summary, insight, recommendation, score, dashboard, or list of options

## What Works

- **Copy-paste ready text.** Every comment, DM, connection request, email must be final-draft quality
- **Green copy buttons on HTML.** Click to copy, paste into target
- **Short commands.** "/q-morning", "posted", "do it", "commented on X"
- **Screenshots as input.** Share a screenshot, system does everything
- **Single recommended action.** Pick the best one. No 3 options to evaluate
- **Intel drops format.** One signal, one person, one copy-paste message. Highest-ROI activity
- **Effort tracking.** "You sent 4 messages today" works. "Nobody responded" does not
- **Auto-detection.** System detects DM replies and connection accepts. Less reporting = better
- **Casual voice.** "man," "dude" (never "bro"), lowercase, conversational

## What Fails

- **Cross-references.** "See section above" is a dead end. Information must be inline
- **Dashboards without actions.** Pipeline counts are meaningless without next actions per person
- **Scores without next steps.** "Hot 8+" is analytics. Needs: the DM text to send
- **Having to decide what to do next.** System must decide. He executes
- **Multi-step processes requiring memory.** System carries state between steps
- **Being asked questions.** Don't ask "would you like me to..." Just present the action
- **Summaries instead of source material.** Full post text required, not one-line paraphrases
- **Walls of text.** Bullet points. Short sentences. Visual breaks
- **Pressure/shame/urgency/guilt language.** Triggers RSD and freeze response
- **Options without recommendations.** Burns decision energy. Pick the best one

## Energy Patterns

- **Flow state:** Investigation, research, data collection, learning. Can hyperfocus for hours
- **Quick Wins first.** Build momentum and dopamine before harder tasks
- **What gets delayed:** Composition, decisions about who to contact, evaluating options
- **What piles up:** Follow-ups, status tracking, remembering who replied

## Interaction Style

- Screenshots to communicate (LinkedIn post -> system identifies person, generates comments)
- Short imperative messages: "do it," "posted," "commented on X"
- Corrections are direct: "You are forgetting who I am"
- Does NOT browse dashboards. Needs one doc that tells him what to do, in order, pre-written

## Core Identity

- Systems thinker and investigator
- Direct, casual, emotionally invested
- Values integrity in positioning and relationships
- The daily HTML is not a briefing. It is his entire workday, pre-loaded and ready to execute
- His AI is not an assistant. It is his external executive function, working memory, follow-up tracker, relationship manager, and copywriter
